About Me

Hello, my name is Garrett Sessoms. I am a graphic designer, artist, and editor from Angier, North Carolina. I am currently in Wake Tech in the Advertising and Graphic Design Program. I have always enjoyed designing and creating things on paper, so I wanted to take my education in that direction. Ever since I can remember, I have been drawing and sketching. I have always known that I was a creative person, and I want my creativity to shine through with whatever job I end up doing.
